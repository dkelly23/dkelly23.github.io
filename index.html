About Me

I'm an Economist from El Colegio de México, currently working as a Data Scientist at Mexico's Agencia de Transformación Digital y Telecomunicaciones. My work is focused on developing web applications using Shiny, JavaScript and PostgreSQL, as well as providing various insights from a variety of public and confidential data sources. I'm comfortable working with micro-econometrics and time-series, and I'm highly experienced in data visualization and visual storytelling. I have strong quantitative and statistical programming skills, specifically in R.

Debating

As part of my academic formation, I also participated on a variety of British Parlamentary Debating championships. I coached the debating team from Instituto Tecnológico Autónomo de México on the academic year 2023-2024. During this academic term, students from ITAM became national Finalist and world Quarter-Finalist.
